iPhone 15 Pro Max

The most advanced iPhone ever made.

Design & Build

The iPhone 15 Pro Max features a stunning titanium frame that's both strong and lightweight. With precision-milled edges and a ceramic shield front, it offers excellent durability and a sleek feel in hand.

Display

The 6.7-inch Super Retina XDR OLED display supports ProMotion with adaptive 120Hz refresh rate, HDR10, and up to 2000 nits brightness for outdoor visibility.

Performance

Powered by the revolutionary 3nm A17 Pro chip, the iPhone 15 Pro Max delivers blazing-fast performance, console-level gaming, and efficient multitasking.

Camera System

Capture life in pro quality with a 48MP main sensor, 12MP ultra-wide, and a 12MP 5x periscope telephoto lens. The Photonic Engine enhances detail and color across all lighting conditions.

Battery & Charging

Get all-day battery life with improved power efficiency from the A17 Pro chip. Enjoy fast charging via USB-C and convenient wireless charging with MagSafe.
